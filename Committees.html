Committees

Our committees, focus on program areas and include board members and community members. Each committee is focused on an area to meet a need of the community.

Housing Committee

Help support our transformative housing programs that provide permanent affordability for tenants, including development of new housing and refurbishment/management of Holly courts and Alemany public housing sites.

Community Engagement Committee

Help support strategic planning, designing, and implementing community engagement programs, including public safety meetings/initiatives, special events, and the annual Bernal Community Congress.

Fiesta Committee

Help plan the annual Fiesta on the Hill, including recruiting sponsors and brainstorming theme and activities.
