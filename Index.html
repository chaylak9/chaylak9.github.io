Donate and Become a Member Today

Bernal Heights is a member based non-profit organization serving the Bernal Heights, Excelsior and surrounding communities. We focus on affordable housing, senior programs, youth and adult employment, community building and engagement, community safety and youth leadership. Support and sustain the awesome work that we provide to our community by becoming a member or making a donation today!
